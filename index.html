How pricing works

  • Estimate from the form — based on your package pick, vehicle size, condition, and selected extras.
  • Final price after we see the car — we confirm the real scope on site before you pay.
  • Travel fee if far — depends on distance from Canton (see table below).
  • Extras only if needed — you only pay for what actually applies.

Weather + reschedule

For mobile work, heavy rain/snow or unsafe conditions can push the start time or move the appointment to the next open slot. If that happens, we message you ASAP and keep you updated.

Deposits (optional)

For long interior jobs or longer travel, we may request a small deposit to hold the spot. If we ever ask, we will explain the amount and refund rules before you agree.

Aftercare (first 24 hours)

  • If seats/carpets were shampooed, keep windows cracked a little when safe.
  • Avoid slamming heavy floor mats back in until fully dry.
  • Light smells right after cleaning can be normal and usually fade quickly.
